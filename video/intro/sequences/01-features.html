> 01_WHY_FRAMV

[ SCALABILITY_GAP ]

Traditional NLE editing cannot be automated at scale. Personalized video for thousands of users requires a programmatic approach.

[ DATA_INTEGRATION ]

Bridging real-time data and video frames is complex. Syncing animations and audio needs an infrastructure, not just a renderer.

[ PLAIN_HTML ]

Write sequences as plain HTML files. No bundler, no framework lock-in. Load framv.js and your HTML becomes a renderable video.

[ CLOUD_RENDER ]

Render in parallel on cloud infrastructure. Headless Chrome captures each frame. Output is production-ready MP4.
